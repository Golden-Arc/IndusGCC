Configure "Advanced Security" from "Security" menu: Select "Packets Statistics Interval" as 30; Set "DoS Protection" to "Enable"; Check "Enable ICMP-FLOOD Attack Filtering" and Set "ICMP-FLOOD Packets Threshold" to 100 packets; Check "Enable UDP-FLOOD Filtering" and Set "UDP-FLOOD Packets Threshold" to 450 packets; Check "Enable TCP-SYN-FLOOD Attack Filtering" and Set "TCP-SYN-FLOOD Packets Threshold" to 60 packets; and finally click "Save".
